    Adomas Juška is a theatre director based in Vilnius.

    He has directed 7 plays, which have been shown in theatres and festivals across Lithuania and abroad (see "Works" section). 6 of which you can still see (see "Calendar" section").

    Some events of varying importance:

    1996 - born in Vilnius, Lithuania.
    2017 - debut as an actor (in "Zinc (Zn)").
    2018 - released first play on the small stage ("Švejk").
    2019 - finished theatre directing studies in in Lithuanian Academy of Music and Theatre (prof. Eimuntas Nekrošius).
    2021 - released first play on the big stage ("Don Quixote")
    2024 - became youngest person to win Golden Stage of the Cross award for Best Director.
